Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2025

1. Introduction

The Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (DRRMO) of Silay City is committed to protecting the privacy and security of all users of the Flood Monitoring System with GIS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our system. By accessing or using this system, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register for an account or use our services, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Contact number
  • User role and organization affiliation
  • Profile photograph (optional)
  • Account credentials (username and encrypted password)

2.2 Flood Assessment Data

When conducting flood hazard assessments or generating certificates, we collect:

  • Establishment name and address
  • Property owner information
  • Location coordinates and barangay
  • Flood susceptibility classifications
  • Assessment dates and mitigation measures

2.3 System Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the system:

  • Activity logs and timestamps
  • IP addresses and browser information
  • Pages visited and features accessed
  • System interactions and form submissions

2.4 Environmental Monitoring Data

Our system collects and processes:

  • Rainfall measurements and patterns
  • Flood incident records
  • Water level data
  • Weather information from external APIs
  • Tidal data for coastal monitoring

3. How We Use Your Information

DRRMO Silay City uses the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Disaster Management: To monitor flood risks, issue alerts, and coordinate emergency responses
  • Service Provision: To process assessment requests, generate certificates, and provide flood hazard information
  • System Administration: To manage user accounts, approve access requests, and maintain system security
  • Record Keeping: To maintain comprehensive records of flood incidents, assessments, and certificates for official purposes
  • Planning and Analysis: To analyze flood patterns, identify high-risk areas, and support disaster preparedness planning
  • Communication: To send notifications, alerts, and important updates regarding flood risks and system information
  • Compliance: To meet legal and regulatory requirements for disaster risk reduction and management

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

DRRMO Silay City may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Government Agencies

We may share information with other government agencies and local government units for disaster management, emergency response coordination, and public safety purposes.

4.2 Emergency Situations

During emergencies, disasters, or life-threatening situations, we may share relevant information with emergency responders, medical personnel, and other authorities to protect lives and property.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or governmental request, or to enforce our policies and protect our rights and the safety of others.

4.4 Public Records

Certain information, such as flood hazard assessments and certificates, may become part of public records in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

4.5 Data Sources

We obtain flood susceptibility and environmental data from authorized government sources including GeoAnalytics, HazardHunter PH, Open-Meteo API, and World Tides.

5. Data Security

DRRMO Silay City implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure user authentication and password encryption
  • Access controls and user permission levels
  • Regular system security updates and maintenance
  • Activity logging and monitoring
  • Secure data storage and backup procedures

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Flood assessment records, certificates, and incident reports are maintained in accordance with government record retention requirements.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Subject to applicable laws and regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information stored in our system
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Update: Update your profile information and account settings
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a commonly used format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your information, subject to legal and operational constraints

Please note that certain information may be retained for legal, regulatory, or operational purposes even after you request deletion or modification.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our system uses session cookies and similar technologies to maintain your login session, remember your preferences, and improve system functionality. These cookies do not store sensitive personal information and are deleted when you close your browser or log out.

9. Third-Party Services

Our system integrates with external data sources and services for weather information, tidal data, and flood risk assessments. These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services.

10. Children's Privacy

This system is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

DRRMO Silay City reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of significant changes through system notifications or email. Your continued use of the system after such modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. Data Protection Officer

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact our Data Protection Officer or the DRRMO Silay City office.
