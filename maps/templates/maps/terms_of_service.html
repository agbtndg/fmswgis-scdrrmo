2. System Purpose and Scope

The Flood Monitoring System with GIS is designed to support disaster risk reduction and management activities in Silay City, including:

  • Monitoring rainfall, flood levels, and environmental conditions

  • Processing flood assessment requests and issuing certificates
  • Maintaining records of flood incidents and disaster events
  • Supporting emergency response coordination and public safety
  • Facilitating data-driven decision-making for disaster preparedness
